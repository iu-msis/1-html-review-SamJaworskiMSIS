Page About Corn

​

Corn is a North American cereal grain that has many purposes. In Indiana we usually see industrial corn, that is used as feed or turned into ethanol. This is different than the corn people normally eat, which is sweet corn. Sweet corn is not only sweeter, but is slightly small, and can be easily eaten raw off the stalk. The corn you see around indiana is not bery tastey, and hurts your teeth to bit into, and if you do end up eating it one ought boil it first.
